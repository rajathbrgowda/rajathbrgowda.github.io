Software Engineering || Data Science || Statistical Machine Learning

I am pursuing my MS in Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California with a specialization in Software Engineering and Data Science. I have also worked on web technologies and process automation at Bharat Electronics.

During my 4 years of employment, I explored the state of technologies in embedded systems and acquired skills in project management, production process strategies, and quality assessment of software and system. I worked on Digital Interface Cards of a Signal processing unit. Additionally, I developed a software program on production process automation using python and a web application for a database management system using MEVN full-stack. My other responsibilities include quality assessment of the software and system as per MIL-STD and JSS55555. During this time, I have been awarded Executive excellence for young achievers.

While working on the process management software, I came across plenty of data from the receiver system. Exploratory data analysis helped cleanse the data and feature extraction for target prediction. Later I started working on machine learning models. The results intrigued me to explore the field of data science. I obtained proficiency certification in data science from the Indian Institute of science. This course helped to understand the core of data science and its application.

Apart from my studies and employment, I have been a part of social service groups and the Science club. I was a student body president of the Science association in my undergrad. We organized the Engineering tech fest, seminars, and research paper presentations built my skills in team and time management, event planning, and communication. Also, I have been awarded Rajyapuraskar(State level) for social service from the National Scouting and Guiding Association of India.
